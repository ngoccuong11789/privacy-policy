Privacy Policy for Jobee App

Effective Date: 19/06/2025

Last Updated: 19/06/2025

This Privacy Policy explains how Jobee (“I”, “me”, or “my”) collects, uses, and protects your personal information when you use the Jobee app on iOS. This app is developed and maintained by an individual, not a company or business entity.

1. Purpose of the App

Jobee helps connect employers (who need help with jobs such as restaurant service, nail technicians, home care, etc.) with Vietnamese job seekers in the U.S. looking for local job opportunities.

2. Information I Collect

  • Name
  • Email address
  • Phone number
  • Location (City/State)
  • Google account info (if used to sign in)
  • Job listing or application data
  • Messages exchanged via the app
  • Device and usage data via Firebase

3. How the Information is Collected

Information is collected when you:

  • Register using email, phone number, or Google login
  • Fill in profile or job details
  • Post or apply to a job
  • Use in-app features such as chat or save jobs

4. How I Use the Data

  • Account management and login
  • Display and manage job listings and applications
  • Facilitate communication between users
  • Improve the app and fix bugs

I do not use your data for advertising or sell your data.

5. Third-Party Tools Used

I use Google Firebase for:

  • Authentication (email, phone, Google)
  • Database and file storage
  • Analytics and crash reporting
  • Push notifications

6. Data Storage and Security

All user data is stored securely using Firebase. I apply encryption and access control. However, no method is 100% secure.

7. Your Rights

You can request to:

  • View or update your profile
  • Delete your account and data
  • Request details of what data I hold

Email your request to: ngoccuong11789@gmail.com

8. Children’s Privacy

This app is not intended for users under 13 years of age. I do not knowingly collect data from children.
